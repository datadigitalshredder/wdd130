Overview

Purpose

The next agricultural season is around the corner. Are you optimistic about a bumper harvest? Are your inputs in place? Cropserve is here to see you through to that end! As a highly diverse and specialized supplier of agricultural inputs, Cropserve provides these agricultural services namely, disease and pest management, plant nutrition, and plant and soil analysis services. These services are at the peak of research and development and carter for Integrated Pest Management principles. With CropTech-Agri as your partner, you’re headed for the most productive season.

Audience

The target audience are the farmers in Zimbabwe that range from small scale to large scale commercial farmers. These individuals are experts in their field with years of farming experience. However, as the technology used in farming keeps changing and improving towards a safer environment, farmers frequently find themselves in need of state-of-the-art farming technologies such as Integrated Pest Management. This site will provide the services that they need to that end through promotion of bioproducts and artificial chemicals that are safer for the environment at a global level. Precision farming is another tool that any farmer, novice or experienced, need for a better yield. Farmers usually access farming sites on mobile devices and on tablets, but rarely on desktops.

CropTech-Agri, Your one-stop agri-solutons partner

CropTech-Agri is a leading precision farming agrochemicals retail company that specializes in provided long term holistic faring solutions to farmers in Zimbabwe. The depth and breath of the services offered by CropTech-Agri is backed up renowned global agrochemicals suppliers. These suppliers are experts in Integrated Pest Management techniques through their cutting-edge innovative measures to ensure eco-friendly production of chemicals. The products available at CropTech-Agri include pesticides, granular and foliar fertilizers, soil conditioners, soil and crop analysis services, and technical support. At CropTech-Agri we ensure that our customers’ needs are met by a team of highly technical and passionate agronomists who are well vest in the science of farming and the use of chemicals.

Pesticides

CropTech-Agri has a wide variety of bio- and synthetic pesticides. These pesticides include insecticides (including pherolures), nematicides, acaricides, fungicides, and herbicides. Other products use hand in hand with these chemicals include plant growth regulators, suckericides, and adjuvants. These products are chemicals that have been designed by principal companies after years of research and trials are manufactured and suppled to Zim-Cropserve by renowned manufacturers around the world. These manufacturers include Sineria Holland BV, King Quenson Industries, Agri-Technovation, UPL, Insect Science, Hygrotech, Ascort Pro-G, and DVA Chemicals. CropTech-Agri is the registered retailer in Zimbabwe of these products. There is guarantee of getting a boost in your harvest as you partner with us in your farming endeavors.

These chemicals are state-of-the-art formulations that are designed to give you the best of your farming practices. This is ensured by development of bio-pesticides instead of the traditional chemicals that have proven to be detrimental to the environment. Examples of these chemicals include Bio-T Plus, a formulation of Bacillus thuringeansis variant that inhibits growth of Lepthedoterian insects. This is a live product and has special storage requirements. Its efficacy has proven to be very cost effective. Other products in this group include, Rhizo-Shield, an insecticide that acts in plant roots, Rhizo-Shield, a soil conditioner. The active ingredients in these products are the natural predators of these pests and are thus safe for use in the environment. By use of these products you are guaranteed not to harm beneficial organism in the ecosystem such as bees. This is how at CropTech-Agri we ensure that as a farmer you are able to implement Integrated Pest Management. In Integrated Pest Management, you only target the pest while promoting the survival of the beneficial insects or organisms. Unlike, using the traditional chemicals which affect other beneficial organisms, in Integrated Pest Management you only control the pest without causing imbalances in the ecosystem.

Crops

In terms of the crops that our chemicals can be applied to, to meet your farming needs, the list would be endless. We carter for all grains such as maize, wheat, and sorghum. We also carter for vegetables such as peppers, potatoes, tomatoes, cucurbits, brassicas, podded vegetables including peas, green beans, soya beans, and field beans. We also ensure fruit farmers are not left out. These include those that farm tree nuts, avocadoes, oranges, pecans, macadamias, apples, bananas, blue berries etc.

These services allow to make world class exports and penetrate high-end markets such as the European market. Our team of agronomists is constantly on the outlook of developments in the international market to make sure you meet the standards of those ever-changing markets. This ensures that you do not use chemicals that have been banned for use in foods intended for human consumption. The costs associated with disposal of condemned foods are very high and that has to be properly controlled.

In terms of plant nutrition, we have traditional granular fertilizers that are applied directly to the soil and we have specific formulations for specific crops. We also have foliar fertilizers that are much effective. We also offer soil and plant analysis services that provide a precise guide to your unique needs as your work towards a good harvest.

With all these services, ranging from pesticides to fertilizers to consultancy services, CropTech-Agri is indeed your One-Stop Agri-Solutions partner.
